# SS Research

Local-first stock research hub for tracking company **stories** the Peter Lynch way — product, customers, people, and a living Stonk sheet.

Clean research coverage UI; process powered by your `Stonk.MD` framework.

## Run

```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open the URL Vite prints (usually `http://localhost:5173`).

## What you get

- **Research coverage grid** — company cards with story blurb, Lynch type, recommendation, sheet progress
- **Story sheet** — full Stonk.MD framework per company (two-minute drill, BS checklist, superior criteria, financials, verdict)
- **Story log** — note when the story changes (Lynch: update the sheet)
- **Principles strip** — always-visible rotating reminders from your principles
- **Principles page** — full framework map + checklists
- **Coverage board** — table scan of every name under coverage
- **Compare** — up to four company stories side by side (no fake charts)
- **Export** — Stonk.MD (clipboard/download) and full JSON import/export
- **Dark mode** · data in `localStorage` only

## Workflow

1. Add a company → write the **two-minute story** first.
2. Fill product / feedback / market lenses.
3. Work the BS checklist and superior-company criteria.
4. Log story updates after earnings or thesis changes.
5. Export Stonk.MD into any LLM when you want a second pass.

## Principles (always on)

- A company is people making a product or service.
- Outcome = vector sum of the people.
- Focus on products & customer experience.
- Update the sheet when the story changes.
- Time is on your side with superior companies.
